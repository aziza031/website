Cinta Dalam Ikhlas

The story of the movie “Cinta Dalam Ikhlas” highlights two young characters named Athar and Ara. The two of them get to know each other and make Athar feel better when he is with Ara. However, they are too young to be in an official relationship and still have dreams to pursue.

Dilan 1990

Dilan 1990 tells the story of Dilan, a mischievous and charismatic young man, who falls in love with Milea, a new girl in Bandung. Dilan in his unique way tries to win Milea's heart, even though their relationship has to face various obstacles. This colorful first love story shows the beauty and challenges of teenage love.

Habibie & Ainun

“Habibie & Ainun” tells the story of true love between BJ Habibie and Ainun. They met when they were young and supported each other in their life journey. When Ainun fell ill, Habibie remained faithful to accompany her. This movie depicts loyalty, love, and sacrifice in the face of life's trials.

Mencuri Raden Saleh

"Mencuri Raden Saleh" tells the story of a group of young men who plan to steal a painting by Raden Saleh. With various conflicts and obstacles, they try to achieve their common goal in an action full of suspense and surprises.
